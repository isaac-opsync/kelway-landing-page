Common Questions

Before you book.

Is this just an AI chatbot?
No. It's 8 integrated workflows covering the full lead-to-revenue lifecycle. The AI conversation is one component. The rest is the operational layer between "lead comes in" and "case completes" — booking, recovery, follow-up, reactivation, reporting.
We already have a CRM. Do we need to switch?
No. Keep it. We build inside the CRM you already use — GHL, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce, whatever. The system acts on your CRM data; it doesn't replace your CRM.
Can the AI give mortgage advice?
No — and it doesn't. The system handles intake, qualification, booking, and follow-up. It never touches regulated advice, product recommendations, rate quotes, or suitability assessments. Your advisers remain in full control of the FCA-regulated process. The AI is positioned as an intake assistant, and that's clearly disclosed to leads.
How long until it's live?
1–2 weeks from signing. Discovery, build, configuration, testing, training, and launch are all included and fully managed by us.
What if it doesn't deliver?
That's what the guarantee is for. 10 extra qualified appointments in month one — or you don't pay the build fee. Founding brokers get a 100% refundable build fee.
I don't have time to implement this.
You don't need to. The build is fully done-for-you. You provide CRM and calendar access; we handle everything else. The whole point is to give your team time back.
How do you handle data and GDPR?
All processing happens inside your existing CRM and our UK/EU-hosted automation layer. We sign a DPA. You own all the data and the workflows.
