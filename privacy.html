Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 April 2026

Kelway Automation ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website and services.

Who We Are

Kelway Automation is a trading name of the Opsync Group. We build automated lead conversion systems for UK mortgage brokers.

Contact: isaac@opsync.co.uk

What We Collect

When you fill out our enquiry form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Number of advisers in your firm

We also collect technical data automatically:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time on site
  • Referral source (how you found us)

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your enquiry via SMS and/or email
  • Qualify whether our services are a good fit for your business
  • Schedule and conduct calls
  • Send appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Improve our website and advertising

We will not sell your data to third parties.

Legal Basis

We process your data based on:

  • Legitimate interest — following up on your enquiry about our services
  • Consent — when you submit the enquiry form, you consent to us contacting you

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate our website and manage enquiries:

  • Go High Level (LeadConnector) — CRM and communication platform (hosts our forms, sends SMS and email)
  • Meta (Facebook) — advertising and conversion tracking via Meta Pixel
  • Google — Google Meet for video calls, Google Calendar for scheduling
  • Vercel — website hosting

Each service has its own privacy policy and processes data in accordance with GDPR.

Cookies and Tracking

We use the Meta Pixel to track website visits and measure advertising performance. This places cookies on your device. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined above, or until you request deletion. If you enquire but don't become a client, we will retain your data for up to 24 months for potential follow-up, after which it will be deleted.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at isaac@opsync.co.uk.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
